Projects going to be implemented

Online Teaching and Video Lectures

Our online teaching and video lecture series are designed to provide women with flexible, accessible learning opportunities from the comfort of their homes. Through engaging video content and interactive online classes, we cover a wide range of subjects, from basic literacy and vocational skills to more specialized training. Each course is curated by experienced educators to ensure practical, real-world applications that empower women to advance in their personal and professional lives.

Assignments and Exercises for Student Women

To reinforce learning, we provide structured assignments and practical exercises aligned with each course. These assignments are designed to be hands-on and relevant, helping women apply new skills to real-world scenarios. By completing these assignments, student women can build a solid foundation and retain the knowledge they gain, enhancing both their confidence and competence.

Project Works & Weekend Meetings and Support Sessions

Project-based learning is a cornerstone of She Learns, She Teaches, encouraging women to work on projects that showcase their skills and creativity. From community-focused initiatives to entrepreneurial projects, women have the opportunity to develop portfolios that can be used to gain employment or start businesses. Projects are collaborative, with guidance from mentors, enabling women to put their knowledge into action and achieve meaningful outcomes.To build a sense of community and offer additional support, we hold weekend meetings and group sessions where women can connect, share experiences, and receive guidance from mentors and peers. These meetings create a supportive network that helps each woman stay motivated, find answers to her questions, and discuss progress on her educational journey. Weekend sessions also include special workshops on topics like financial literacy, health, and personal growth.
