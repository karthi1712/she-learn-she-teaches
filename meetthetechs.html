Internet Access & Device (Computer, Smartphone, or Tablet)

A stable internet connection is needed to access the online courses, video lectures, and other digital resources available on the platform. This ensures that learners can participate in lessons, assignments, and project work without interruption.
Learners will need a device such as a computer, smartphone, or tablet to access the website and participate in courses. The device should be able to support video playback and online interactions.

Basic Computer Skills & A Valid Email Address

While we provide support and training in digital literacy, a basic understanding of how to navigate a computer or smartphone, use the internet, and send/receive emails can be helpful for some participants. For those who need additional help, we provide introductory courses to enhance these skills.
An email address is required for registration, communication, and receiving updates about new courses, assignments, and important platform notifications. It also helps with personalized progress tracking and feedback.

Commitment and Willingness to Learn & Access to Learning Materials

A strong desire to learn, improve, and take on new challenges is essential. Participants should be motivated to complete assignments, attend meetings, and actively engage in their educational journey.
All the necessary course materials, reading resources, and tools will be provided via the platform. However, learners may need a notebook or digital document editor to complete assignments and project work.
